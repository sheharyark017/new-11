WHO WE ARE

Al Noor Al Nafi Foodstuff Trading LLC, Head quartered in Ajman, United Arab Emirates has grown to occupy a pre-eminent position in logistics network of agri foodstuffs, including oil, rice, spices, sugar, pulses, dry fruits, milk powder, dairy products, Himalayan salt and Tret. With years of experience in sustaining the vibrant international sourcing network for food and refreshments of highest quality standards around the world, we strive to be one of the most trusted sources for healthy food in UAE.

Despite the large network of suppliers over the globe, we have ensured that varied needs of individual customer are constantly met. Thus, our unwavering commitment to building the long lasting relationship with buyers and sellers is the main reason why we have a global reputation of trust and integrity with a network of thousands of customers and suppliers.

Through our multiple processing units and wide area network, we also boost up the brand identity. Apart from this, we always strive to provide an unrivalled choice of food and highest quality products to our valuable customers at affordable price with hassle free shipment. Al Noor Al Nafi Foodstuff meet the national brands on quality, taste and of course price in order to maintain the healthy relationship with its customers.
